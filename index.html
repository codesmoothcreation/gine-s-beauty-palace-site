About Gina's Beauty Palace

Founded by Gina in 2010, our beauty palace has grown from a small nail studio to a full-service beauty destination. We're passionate about helping our clients look and feel their best.

Our team of certified beauty professionals stays current with the latest techniques and trends in the industry. We use only premium, professional-grade products that are safe for all skin types.

At Gina's Beauty Palace, we believe beauty should be an enjoyable, pampering experience. Our luxurious salon environment is designed to help you relax while we work our magic.
